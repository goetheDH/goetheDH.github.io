Mozart's Family

His Family

The Mozart family was descended from Johann Georg Mozart, a bookbinder who lived in Augsburg (Germany). He was the grandfather of the composer Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, who was married to Constanze Weber. The Weber family included the three sisters of Constanze, who were professional singers, and the two families were closely related.
